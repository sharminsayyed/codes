    About Us

    Know About Our Restaurant

    A Legacy of Elegance and Exquisite Flavors Since 1994

    At Blue Nile, where tradition meets innovation, we believe in the magic of bringing people together over delicious food with an unforgettable view. Nestled in the heart of Pune since 1994, Blue Nile offers a cozy and inviting space for a culinary journey like no other.

    • Our founders Sunil Chauhan & Joseph Marron , envisioned a place where the community could come together to enjoy delectable dishes inspired by both local and international cuisine.
    • In our kitchen, only the freshest and finest ingredients are used to craft each dish with care and precision. From our mouth-watering appetizers to our succulent main courses and delightful desserts
    • Our friendly staff and warm ambiance will make you feel right at home.We look forward to serving you soon
